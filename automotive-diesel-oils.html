Automotive & Diesel Oils

Premium engine oils engineered for superior protection in both gasoline and diesel vehicles—from city commute to long-haul trucking.

Premium Performance for Every Vehicle

Finolube automotive and diesel oils are engineered to meet the diverse demands of modern vehicles. Whether you drive a compact sedan, an SUV, or operate a commercial truck fleet, our formulations deliver consistent performance and extended engine life.

Our products are developed using cutting-edge refinery technology and advanced additives that provide protection against wear, oxidation, and thermal degradation under all driving conditions.

Why Finolube Automotive Oils?

Exceptional Fuel Economy

Low-viscosity formulations reduce friction for improved mileage and lower emissions.

Extended Drain Intervals

Superior oxidation stability allows longer service intervals, reducing maintenance costs.

Robust Cleanliness

Advanced detergents and dispersants keep engines clean from deposits and sludge.

Effective Corrosion Protection

Prevents rust and oxidation of critical engine components.

Diesel Engine Strength

Formulated to handle high-pressure diesel engines and demanding conditions.

Cold Start Performance

Maintains flow in cold climates for reliable winter operation.

Vehicle-Specific Performance

For Diesel Vehicles: Specially formulated to handle the unique demands of diesel engines, including high compression ratios and extended fuel injection intervals.

For CNG Vehicles: Optimized for the specific requirements of compressed natural gas engines with enhanced deposit control.
