About Us

FINOLUBE is an established name in the Indian Petroleum Scene, today ranking amongst the Country's leading Manutacturers of Lubricants and Specialties confirming to high quality standards. we have modern facilities and infrastructure at our plant located at VAPI which uses sophisticated technology to produce an impressive range of premium quality products from Industrial Automotive Oils and Petroleum Specialities. FINOLUBE products meet the most stringent of the requirements of the customers. Liberalization has taught us to operate in a global economy in which parameters of quality, delivery, customer satisfaction and other operating factors are indicators of success.

At FINOLUBE, oil is biended from selected high quality base oil and imported additives to manufacture world class lubricant. We have a state-of-the-art laboratory to ensure monitor all the quality aspects right from raw material input to finished blended products. We have developed a wide range of optimum performance lubricants, greases and engine oil for both the automotive and industrial sectors. The entire range of products are environment friendly that ensures full value for money Our stringent quality control system and strong R&D base and endeavour towards qualities upgradation & product development has helped us maintain a lang term relationship with all our existing clients who depends on our experience for their critical needs.
