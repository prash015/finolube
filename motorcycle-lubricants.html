Why Choose Finolube Motorcycle Oils?

Finolube's motorcycle lubricants are specially formulated to meet the demanding requirements of modern two-wheelers. Our products are engineered with advanced additives that provide superior protection, enhanced fuel efficiency, and extended engine life.

Whether you're riding a high-performance sports bike or a daily commuter, our range of motorcycle oils delivers consistent performance, from cold starts in winter to extreme summer conditions.

Application Guidelines

Engine Type Compatibility: Always check your motorcycle owner's manual for the recommended oil grade and API/JASO classification. Using the correct oil ensures optimal engine performance and longevity.

Change Interval: Follow manufacturer recommendations, typically between 2,000-5,000 km depending on riding conditions and engine type.

Storage: Store in cool, dry places away from direct sunlight. Use within 2 years of manufacturing date for best results.
