Finolube bike oil range is engineered for modern 4-stroke motorcycles and scooters under severe riding conditions.

  • Ranger 4T: strong high-speed protection with excellent clutch and gearbox behavior.
  • Drift 4T SM: PBM technology for performance, power and protection.
  • Classic 15W50: synthetic technology blend for durability at high temperature.

Typical pack sizes: 800ml, 900ml, 1L, 1.2L, 20L, 50L, 210L.

Fork Oil is designed for front fork suspension assemblies to deliver smoother ride quality and damping control.

  • Excellent seal compatibility for fork components.
  • Protection against wear, oxidation, corrosion and foaming.
  • Suitable for daily riding and competition use.

Typical pack sizes: 175ml, 350ml.

DOT 3 and DOT 4 brake fluids are formulated for braking and hydraulic clutch systems across vehicle types.

  • High equilibrium reflux boiling point for stable brake feel.
  • Excellent lubricity and corrosion protection for hydraulic components.
  • Resistant to oxidation and thermal degradation.

Suitable for 2/3 wheelers, passenger cars, commercial vehicles and tractors.

Car engine oils use advanced base oils and additives to control sludge and protect modern engines.

  • Tracer 5W30 with Flexi Molecule Technology for cleaner engines.
  • Synthup 5W40 and Magna 10W40 options for varied driving profiles.
  • Optimized cold start and high temperature durability.

Recommended for modern petrol, diesel and CNG passenger cars.

Coolex Coolant 1:4 helps maintain engine temperature and protects radiator systems.

  • Ethylene glycol base with anti-corrosion package.
  • Supports winter and summer operating conditions.
  • Easy miscibility with water using recommended dilution.

Typical pack sizes: 1L, 3L, 5L, 20L, 210L.

Truck engine oil range supports turbo charged diesel engines in on-highway and off-highway operations.

  • Turbo Plus 15W40 for multipurpose commercial diesel use.
  • Road Star 20W40 for deposit control and durability.
  • Maintains viscosity stability in severe duty cycles.

Suitable for medium and heavy commercial applications.

Tractor engine oils are built for agricultural equipment that runs under load and varied weather.

  • Formulated for strong cleanliness and wear control.
  • Stable performance in high temperature and dusty conditions.
  • Compatible with modern tractor engine requirements.

Used in farm-duty engines and allied utility operations.

Gear oil portfolio is designed for transmission and axle systems operating under high pressure loads.

  • Gear EP grades support shock-load protection.
  • Gear Guard 75W90 for superior anti-wear and anti-scuffing performance.
  • Thermal stability helps extend gear and bearing life.

Available for manual transmission and axle applications.

Aerosol and car care products support preventive maintenance and smoother operation of vehicle components.

  • Chain lube spray for improved lubrication coverage.
  • Specialty products for workshop and service needs.
  • Designed to support cleaner operation and reliable performance.

Useful for periodic maintenance and service intervals.

CNG engine oil options support dual-fuel and gas-operated engines with strong viscosity reserve.

  • Royal 4T 20W50 and CNG Special 20W50 for adverse conditions.
  • Advanced additives for lower wear and improved fuel efficiency.
  • Helps maintain cleaner internals and oil burn-off control.

Recommended where 20W50 grade performance is required.

Industrial lubricants include hydraulic and circulation oils for equipment reliability and uptime.

  • Ultra Hydrol AW oils for anti-wear hydraulic requirements.
  • Good foam control, demulsibility and oxidation resistance.
  • Suitable for industrial, construction, agriculture and mining systems.

Grades include 32, 46 and 68 for different duty profiles.

Finolube grease range is lithium soap-based and fortified for long relubrication intervals.

  • Long Life Jelly, Access AP-3 and Vintage AP-3 for wide operating ranges.
  • Water resistance, corrosion protection and thermal stability.
  • Supports bearings and moving parts in adverse conditions.

Pack sizes include retail packs and industrial barrels.
